Ella's Life and Experiences

Hello! My name is Ella Nanto. I am 19 years old, and I am from Gainesville, Virginia- about 40 minutes from D.C., for those who don't know where that is. I attend school at Brigham Young University, and intend to study Information Systems at the Marriott School of Business.
